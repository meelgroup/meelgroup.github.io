Three Papers accepted at <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/cavconference/?fref=mentions">Computer Aided Verification (CAV)</a> 2020 conference.<br>1. The first paper proposes a new approach that combines sampling+machine learning+MaxSAT to achieve a significant progress in solving Boolean Functional Synthesis. Authors: Priyanka Golia, Subhajit Roy, and Kuldeep S. Meel<br>2. The second paper builds on our CNF-XOR solving paradigm (BIRD) and as a result, the new versions of ApproxMC and UniGen are faster than ever. Stay tuned for our releases. Authors: Mate Soos, Stephan Gocht, and Kuldeep S. Meel<br>3. The third paper proposes the first algorithm for approximate MUS counting. Authors: Jaroslav Bendik and Kuldeep S. Meel
