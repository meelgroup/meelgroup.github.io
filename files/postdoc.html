Post-Doctoral Positions for Provably Verified and Explainable Probabilistic Reasoning

Multiple post-doctoral research positions available in the project on "Provably Verified and Explainable Probabilistic Reasoning," focused on developing formal methods for AI techniques and also employ advances in AI techniques for the development of formal methods.
Broadly, the project is sub-divided into four major themes (detailed description available at (https://meelgroup.github.io/#research) https://meelgroup.github.io/#research):

Theme 1: Revisiting NP Revolution: New Paradigms for SAT/SMT/MaxSAT Solvers

Theme 2: Beyond NP Revolution: Constrained Sampling and Integration/Counting

Theme 3: Verification of AI Systems

Theme 4: Interpretable and Explainable Explanations for AI Systems

We work at the intersection of algorithmic design and systems; therefore, an ideal candidate should have deeper expertise in one area and willingness to learn the other. A strong background in statistics, algorithms/formal methods and prior experience in coding are crucial to make a significant contribution to our research.

Two post-doctoral research fellow positions in the area of SAT/CP/AI are available at the School of Computing (http://www.comp.nus.edu.sg), National University of Singapore, Singapore.

The research will be primarily in the broad areas of SAT and CP with relevance to Artificial Intelligence. Some areas of focus include sampling and discrete integration techniques, optimization techniques, modelling, and machine learning for CP/SAT. The positions will be for 1 year with appointment date in 2019 but a longer period may be possible.

Job requirements:

1. A Ph.D. in CS or related disciplines such as ECE, Mathematics, and OR.
2. Research experience in implementing solvers, modeling problems, and applying SAT/CP techniques.

Procedure:
Interested candidates should email meel+postdoc@comp.nus.edu.sg with a PDF of CV, which must contain information of at least two references. Furthermore, a short write up indicating your interest in a particular theme is required.

The initial term of appointment will be one year extensible for another year, upon review of satisfactory performance.

The selected candidates will be offered competitive salaries and benefits.
